An e-commerce website, short for "electronic commerce website," is an online platform that allows businesses and individuals to buy and sell products or services over the internet. These websites facilitate the entire transaction process, from product browsing and selection to payment processing and order fulfillment. E-commerce websites have become increasingly popular and essential in the digital age, as they offer numerous advantages for both consumers and businesses.
